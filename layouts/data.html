What is Autism?

It is a Neurodevelopmental Disorder that affects the social interaction, communication and behavior of the person with this condition of life

What is Ástra?

A specialized institution that provides comprehensive care to people with Autism and other Neurodevelopmental Disorders, from 18 months to adult life through different therapeutic and educational programs. With more than 20 years of experience we have managed to form a team of professionals and an educational model based on good practices of intervention focused on the person, with a family systemic approach and the rights of people with disabilities.

Promover el bienestar de las personas con trastornos del espectro autista, persiguiendo la inclusión a su núcleo familiar, a la sociedad y a medios educativos y laborales regulares.

Promote the welfare of people with autism spectrum disorders, pursuing inclusion to their family nucleus, society and regular educational and work environments

Formar parte del proyecto de vida de las familias con un miembro con autismo, ofreciendo apoyo oportuno de acuerdo a las necesidades con relación a su edad

To be an institution with avant-garde proposals that provide quality and warmth in the care of children with autism and contribute to the strengthening of other public and private institutions. Ante la falta de instituciones y recursos humanos calificados para la atención de personas con autismo en Quintana Roo, se funda Astra el 24 de octubre de 1997 por iniciativa de un grupo de padres con hijos con autismo. Inicia actividades en Cancún en un Centro de Atención Múltiple de la SEP, capacitando a profesionales de la salud y educación a través de colaboraciones con la Clínica Mexicana del Autismo e impartiendo terapias, conformando en poco tiempo los inicios del Programa Psicopedagógico para el Desarrollo. En el año 2000 edifica sus propias instalaciones en un terreno ofrecido en comodato por el Ayuntamiento de Benito Juárez, incrementando la oferta de servicios con el programa Centro Escolar Astra para niños y niñas con autismo. En 2005 crea el Programa Casa Astra: Una Escuela Para la Vida, donde se imparte educación especial a jóvenes y adultos con autismo y discapacidad intelectual para la vida independiente. En 2008 inicia el programa de desarrollo deportivo y artístico. En 2009 se funda el Programa de Detección e Intervención Temprana para Infantes con Autismo. En 2012 se apertura el Programa de Inclusión Educativa de niños y niñas con autismo en el aula ordinaria.

In the absence of institutions and qualified human resources to care for people with autism in Quintana Roo, Astra was founded on October 24, 1997 on the initiative of a group of parents with children with autism. Initiates activities in Cancun in a Multiple Care Center of the SEP, training health professionals and education through collaborations with the Mexican Autism Clinic and providing therapies, forming in a short time the beginnings of the Psychopedagogical Program for Development. In 2000 he built his own facilities in a land offered as a loan by the Benito Juárez City Council, increasing the offer of services with the Astra School Center program for children with autism. In 2005 he created the Casa Astra Program: A School for Life, where special education is given to young people and adults with autism and intellectual disability for independent living. In 2008, the sports and artistic development program began. In 2009, the Early Detection and Intervention Program for Infants with Autism was founded. In 2012, the Program of Educational Inclusion of children with autism in the ordinary classroom was opened.

El órgano máximo de la organización es la asamblea general de socios, integrada por socios fundadores, adjuntos, consejeros, benefactores y adherentes, quienes eligen para su administración a una Junta Directiva integrada por un presidente y representante legal, secretario, tesorero y vocalías con comisiones específicas. Los estatutos de Astra establecen que ninguno de los socios puede recibir remuneración económica. Cualquier benefactor de Astra, si es de su interés, puede ser miembro de la organización con derecho para ser informado, opinar sobre el programa de trabajo y votar por los miembros que integrarán la Junta Directiva.

The maximum organ of the organization is the general assembly of partners, composed of founding partners, deputies, counselors, benefactors and adherents, who elect for its administration a Board of Directors composed of a president and legal representative, secretary, treasurer and committee members. specific. The Astra statutes provide that none of the partners can receive economic compensation. Any Astra benefactor, if it is of interest, can be a member of the organization with the right to be informed, to comment on the work program and to vote for the members that will make up the Board of Directors.
